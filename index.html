How the algorithm works
Complexity score (1–10)
Weighted hours across CoS, Direct Sales, BD&T, and Training (Internal) are normalised against a reference site of ~1,500 weighted hrs/year = score 5. Higher hours and a higher proportion of DS and BD&T push the score up. Scores are clamped 1–10. When filtering by FY or quarter, hours are annualised to a full-year equivalent so complexity is comparable across periods.
FTE calculation
Score × 327 hrs/yr = total annual hours required at that site. The role-mix split allocates those hours to Engineer vs Technician. Dividing by 1,634 net billable hrs/FTE/year gives headcount. Totals across all sites give the portfolio requirement.
